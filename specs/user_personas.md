# Personas

## Host

The person running the game. This person will be on a TV and will be using a mouse to navigate the game. 

## Player

The player participating in the trivia game. 

This person will be on their smartphone and will be using their phone to answer questions and buzz in. 

They will want to be able to see the current question on their phone and be able to buzz in when they know the answer. 

They will also be able to see their current score and the current round and category.
